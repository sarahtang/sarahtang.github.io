As part of the design contract team in Mobile Developers of Berkeley, I spent 3 months in the spring of 2018 as a UX Product Manager and completed both Android mobile designs and conducted user focus groups to improve the experience of using Switchboard.

Switchboard is a Berkeley startup dedicated to creating connections between their users by delivering a mobile app that instantly connects users. Mobile Developers of Berkeley partnered with Switchboard to design and create the Android mobile app as well as conduct user research.
