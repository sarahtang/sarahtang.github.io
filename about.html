Hi! I'm Sarah, a rising junior at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science & Conservation and Resource Studies.

This summer, I am redesigning the developer experience at Trusted Key, a startup that provides secure digital identity solutions using blockchain.

During the semester, I am an associate consultant with BEACN, the premier environmental and corporate consulting club at UC Berkeley. Additionally, I collaborated with Switchboard, a cross-platform app that utilizes voice communication to help teams work effectively, to develop their Android designs as UX Product Manager for Mobile Developers of Berkeley.

As a designer, what never ceases to amaze me is the concept of degrees of separation and how close knit humanity is. My goal is to create products that create empathy while generating compassion and social movement. I strive to bring these elements together in my projects.

In my spare time, you can find me tiramisu tasting, exploring the abundant nature in the Bay Area, and going on spontaneous adventures. I enjoy discussing everything from design to the most recent episodes of The Good Place. I would love to talk over a cup of coffee so definitely reach out!
